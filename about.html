About Us

Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Incorporated Kutztown University Associate Chapter
was founded on September 26, 2021 by founders Mikey Martinez and Eli Perez
to promote culture and diversity among the campus of Kutztown University.

Fraternity Ideals

Our five fraternity ideals that have connected others and created a good image about us are:

  1. Academics
    -Throughout the fraternity's history, academic excellence has always been our number one priority
    when it came to strivig as a chapter to have a good luck on campus.
  2. Leadership
    -We want our brothers to strive not only at school, but also in their academic field.
    We teach and show what is like to have leadership throughout the fraternity.
  3. Community Service
    -Throughout multiple chapters of the fraternity, we love to give back to our community.
    whether that is in our hometown or within our sector, we always give back.
  4. Culture
    -Lambda Theta Phi is a non-exclusive latin fraternity open to all with different backgrounds
    and all share each other's cultures and pride which is what makes us unique.
