About Us

Lambda Theta Phi Latin Fraternity, Incorporated Kutztown University Associate Chapter was founded on September 26, 2021 by founders Mikey Martinez and Eli Perez to promote culture and diversity among the campus of Kutztown University.

Fraternity Ideals

Our five fraternity ideals that have connected others and created a positive image of us are:

Academics
Throughout the fraternity's history, academic excellence has always been our number one priority. We strive as a chapter to maintain strong academic performance and represent ourselves positively on campus.
Leadership
We encourage our brothers to succeed not only in the classroom, but also in their future careers. Throughout the fraternity, we teach and demonstrate what it is like to have strong leadership.
Community Service
Giving back to the community is one of our core values of the fraternity. Whether that is in our hometowns, on campus, or within our sector, we are always committed to give back to the community.
Culture
Lambda Theta Phi is a non-exclusive Latin fraternity open to all individuals of all backgrounds. We celebrate and share our cultures and pride which is what makes us unique.
Brotherhood
With having over 150+ undergraduate and alumni entities, the brotherhood creates lifelong bonds that extend far beyond college and connects brothers throughout the country.
